Usability and Website purpose

The ten heuristics

For this course, we learnt about the ten website usability heuristics. These ten guidelines help website companies ensure that their users have good experiences on their website.

Though often they don't always follow these conventions, so it makes it hard for users and the companies to use properly.

We had a whole mid year exam on this.

Identifying Heuristics

We also learnt about website purpose and their user psychographics. This is essentially what the website is for and who it caters to.

We learnt to scan websites to see what heuristics they followed well, and what could be improved upon.

They were mostly shopping websites.

Example question and answer from exam
